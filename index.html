Arthur Nery
Arthur Nery is a known Filipino singer and songwriter. He was born on January 28, 1997 in Cagayan de Oro with both parents as also singers. He studied in Xavier University and majored in Psychology, this was also the time he started composing songs seriously. He specializes in R&B, Soul, and OPM Genres.
